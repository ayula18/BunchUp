    About Us
    A new way to an old tradition BunchUp is a mobile application that allows for instant proximate social connectivity. The BunchUp app offers meetup solutions by connecting people around the world. Our platform provides a place where people can enlist outing plans and go out with random people whenever and wherever they want. Join the revolution of togetherness and build strong communities with BunchUp. BunchUp was founded in Dec 2022 by Ayush Lahoti and Tanmay Godha with a mission to bring people together.
    Our Vision
    Considering the time teenagers are spending on social media to connect digitally, BunchUp feels there is a huge opportunity in creating content for teens. We intend to provide our users a platform for them to plan and execute meetup/outing plans, with people of their interest. Great emphasis on creating a safe place for people to socialize and hangout with others, let it café hopping, clubbing or movie night. Don't let your plans get cancelled.
