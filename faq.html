    FAQs
    Imagine it's a long weekend and you wanna go out with your friends. When you call them up you find everyone is either busy or they just ditched you. Panic? Frustrated? Cancelling the plans? Dont worry download BunchUp now and enlist your outing plans and hangout with people of your interest. That's not it you can join other people’s plans too and get yourself included in it. Let it be Cafe Hopping, Clubbing Or a Movie Plan just BunchUp…
    The BunchUp team is continuously working on the application. We will be launching it within the next 3 months. The app then can be downloaded from Google Play Store and App Store. All the links and updates will be given on the website itself.
    BunchUp will have many exciting features as follow:
    1)Enlisting your outing plans.
    2)Joining other people's plans.
    3)Build your profile and collect Buncoin to redeem them with discounts and offers.
    3)Organising meetups and getting discounts on Cafes, Clubs and Restaurants.
    4)You can organise house parties.
    5)You can promote your own event.
    BunchUp connects you with people using location-based technology based on the distance you set as per your travelling limits. Also you can set filters for people who will be joining you. Filters include your interests in activities, Educational background or Age groups etc.. You will get a group chat box differently for every event so that you can contact every attendee.
    BunchUp will offer Photo Verification to make sure that there are no catfish profiles on the application. With this you get to see the person’s profile which will include a photo verification tick and different profile levels. With this you can filter out yourself with whom you want to plan the outing, as the outing host will get authority to approve the attendee. All these safety measures will create the platform safe and authenticated.
    First of all there are no applications which provide this kind of service which BunchUp is going to provide. There are a few similar applications but they are either very expensive to afford or they are not very user friendly for the Indian audience. Considering all these drawbacks we got our inspiration to build a platform like BunchUp.
